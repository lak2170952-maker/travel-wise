# Veteran Support Network

Welcome to the Veteran Support Network website. This platform is dedicated to providing essential resources and support for military veterans, including housing, employment, mental health, and benefits guidance.

## Features
- **Housing Support:** Information on emergency shelters, transitional housing, and long-term housing programs.
- **Employment & Training:** Access to job boards, resume tools, and training programs for veterans.
- **Mental Health & Wellness:** Counseling, peer support groups, and crisis resources tailored to veterans.
- **Benefits Navigation:** Guidance on VA benefits, disability claims, and financial assistance.
